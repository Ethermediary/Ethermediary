Ethermediary
Ethermediary is an Ethereum based smart contract. It allows you to safely trade objects and services for money on the internet, and accepts Bitcoin, Ether, and every other ERC20 tokens available.

Ethermediary acts as a safe third party, completely independant and trustless, which garanty the seller to be paid, and the buyer to received the object or service for close to nothing.
